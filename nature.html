Nature photography

Intention

Nature photography is considered by the public to be one of the easiest categories of work to produce, you just need good equipment as well as perfect time planning and the ability to go to the right location and you will be able to synthesize a perfect nature photograph. But different people's nature photography also discloses the mood of the creator and the uniqueness of the mind behind that lens.

I enjoy photographing nature because my memory will one day become less clear, and one day I will forget details about the mountains or the oceanor the sun. Then this is a time when nature photography can always serve as a guiding light in the maze of my memory.
