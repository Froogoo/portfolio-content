Landscape photography

Although there are numerous similar flowers, the one I pressed the shutter on was still unique.

Nature photography is considered by the public to be one of the easiest categories of work to produce, you just need good equipment as well as perfect time planning and the ability to go to the right location and you will be able to synthesize a perfect nature photograph. But different people's nature photography also discloses the mood of the creator and the uniqueness of the mind behind that lens. It is because in the age of digital photography, there is no longer any difference between the subject and the reproduction. Photography of landscapes, documentaries, and portraits needs to prove that these images are produced by the "I" as the dominant force. How to naturally leave "dominance" in a photograph becomes the challenge of nature photography today.

I enjoy photographing nature because my memory will one day become less clear, and one day I will forget details about the mountains or the ocean and the sun. Then this is a time when nature photography can always serve as a guiding light in the maze of my memory. Nature photography for me is no longer an act of photography that requires a motivation, it more likes playing a game on a computer, but other genres of photography that requires intention is like doing homework on a computer. I can't copy other people's tracks, so I can make the one I captured full of uniqueness.
