Portraits

"Selfies" took the top spot in the image ranking.

Portrait photography is a type of documentary, and I hope to clearly capture all my interpersonal relationships. Although the photograph shows other people, the actual story is played out by the photographer and the subject. In the era of image poverty, people were afraid of the camera taking their souls, or they were willing to show their faces more to fight for the right to take pictures. Now, with the unrestricted right to image, people can focus on everything and shoot everything, but they are trapped in the difficulty of making the “perfect” portrait.

Street Photograph

You are recording the field as an audience in public space.

It is difficult to have a clear definition of street photography, the images it embodies will change with the times and local culture. For me personally, I feel something on the street that I find interesting, something aesthetically pleasing, something with a coherent theme, and this is what makes me want to press the shutter, or maybe this is Snapshot. Street photography will show how I move and act in a space and what I care most about around me. Street photography can also be a documentation of small random happenings in human history. This kind of photography is really an "extension of my entity", but such an extension makes me return to a more lonely two-dimensional world.
